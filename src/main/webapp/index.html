Pramod Maharana

Assistent Art Director + Short film maker

A Journey towards industry . . . .

Toggle Video   Get Started

There is no substitute for hard work, trust me when I say that. I have been around for quite some time in the entertainment industry, persevering everyday towards my goal of competing with no one but myself and growing immensely in this process. I have started from scratch - from doing TVCs to an interesting potpourri of fiction and reality TV shows. Today, I feel extremely humbled by all the love and appreciation that I get from you all! It feels great when my friends and admirers acknowledge me as a hardworking actor. It is good to be appreciated for my looks and physique, but it is my work that I want to be known for. The truth is that I was not tailor-made for this profession. I am just another regular guy like one of you. Coming from Delhi, I had no aspirations to become an actor initially. I was about to get into my father's business of stock brokerage when destiny called and I moved to Mumbai. I have had my share of hardships and I actually started acting to pay my rent, but was soon sucked into the creativity of the profession and never looked back! 'From Lucky To Saraswatichandra and now, MahaKumbh, Its been one hell of a journery!
